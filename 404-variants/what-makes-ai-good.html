🥇 What Makes an AI Good? (Top Qualities)

  • 1. Accuracy & Reliability Hallucination-free, fact-checked responses. Handles edge cases.
  • 2. Context Awareness Long context (128k+ tokens), remembers conversation, adapts.
  • 3. Speed & Efficiency Low latency (<1s), scales to high throughput.
  • 4. Tool Integration Calls APIs/tools (code exec, search, files) for real-world actions.
  • 5. Reasoning & Planning Step-by-step thinking, multi-step tasks, error correction.
  • 6. Versatility Multimodal (text/image/code), 50+ langs, domains (code, math, creative).
  • 7. Safety & Alignment No harmful output, bias mitigation, user control.
  • 8. Usability Natural UX, personalization, cost-effective.
  • 9. Transparency Explains reasoning, citations, uncertainty admission.
  • 10. Innovation Agentic (self-improving), custom fine-tunes.

Good AI solves problems better than humans in specific domains. Balance all!
